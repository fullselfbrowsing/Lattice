<objective>
Wire the Plan 01 sidecar primitives into the two CLI handlers (`lattice repro`, `lattice eval`) and the eval runner so the v1.1 audit limitations actually close. The repro and eval surfaces honor the sidecar convention (`.lattice/sidecars/<receipt-id>.json`) by default and accept explicit `--sidecar <path>` / `--sidecar-dir <dir>` flags. Same flag names on both subcommands for consistency.

Purpose: With Plan 01 the loader exists but nothing reads it. Plan 02 makes `lattice repro` actually replay to a `verdict=match` and makes `lattice eval` actually fire the cost-regression gate. Both behaviors are what the v1.1-MILESTONE-AUDIT.md flagged as forward-compat carryovers; Phase 13.1 closes them.

Output:
- `runRepro` reads a sidecar (explicit path, then convention, then fallback) and threads `{ task, outputs, policy, contract }` into `materializeReplayEnvelope`. Exit 0 on match; exit 1 on drift; exit 2 on any earlier load/verify/materialize failure (unchanged matrix).
- `runEval` walks `walkReceiptsWithSidecars` instead of `walkReceiptsDirectory`. Each fixture's sidecar (when present) is applied to materialize; when absent, the fixture's `FixtureReport.loadFailedReason` is `"no-sidecar"` instead of generic `load-failed`.
- `EvalConfig.sidecarsDir` is a first-class field with a default of `.lattice/sidecars`.
- `FixtureReport.loadFailedReason` is a typed discriminator that the e2e test (Plan 03) and the audit can read.
</objective>

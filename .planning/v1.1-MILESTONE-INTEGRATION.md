### Unprotected Routes

### Documented v1.1 Limitations (TECH-DEBT, intentional, deferred to v1.2)

1. **`lattice repro` returns `execution_unavailable` against a receipt-only envelope** — The receipt body does NOT embed `task` / `outputs` / `policy`, so `materializeReplayEnvelope` produces a `ReplayEnvelope` without `outputs`, and `replayOffline` returns `execution_unavailable`. Documented in 13-02-SUMMARY.md "Known Limitations" #1 and asserted as expected behavior by showcase-e2e.test.ts case 3. v1.2 will add sidecar-outputs support and the assertion will flip to `verdict=match`.

2. **`lattice eval` cost-regression gate cannot fire in v1.1** — Same root cause as #1: every showcase fixture hits `load-failed` at runner stage 4, so the cost comparator never runs. Documented in 13-02-SUMMARY.md "Known Limitations" #2 and exercised in showcase-e2e.test.ts case 6 with forward-compat conditional (accepts exit 0 OR exit 1). v1.2 will lift this boundary automatically.

Both limitations are pre-disclosed in Phase 13's SUMMARY, accepted by the milestone goal as "v1.1 wedge", and have forward-compat test scaffolding that will exercise the new behavior once v1.2 ships — without test rewrite.
